Curriculum

We offer a structured academic journey that adapts to every stage of a studentâ€™s growth.

  • Structured Learning with Clear Progression: Our school follows the State Secondary School Certificate (SSC) curriculum, recognized and administered by the Andhra Pradesh Board of Secondary Education.   • Focus on Foundational Skills: The SSC system is designed to strengthen literacy, numeracy, and analytical abilities at every stage, ensuring readiness for higher education or vocational paths.
  • Continous and Comprehensive Education: Assessment includes formative (internal) and summative (term-end) evaluations that encourage:
    • Active participation
    • Regular feedback

  • SSC Board Exam Preparation: From Grade 9 onward, students are gradually trained for the SSC Board Examinations, with:
    • Sample paper practice
    • Time management drills
    • Doubt-clearing sessions
    • Revision-focused scheduling
  • Value-Integrated Education: In addition to academics, the curriculum emphasizes:
    • Moral Science and Life skills
    • Physical Education
    • Art and Culture
    • Environmental Awareness
