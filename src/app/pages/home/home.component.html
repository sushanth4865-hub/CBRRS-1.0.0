About Our CBRR School:

Chaitanya Bharathi Root & Rise School has proudly stood as a beacon of learning for over 33 years, rooted deeply in traditional values while fostering the rise of every child into a confident, capable individual. Unlike institutions that treat education as a business, we see our school as a sacred opportunity to shape lives, empower potential, and support students in achieving meaningful milestones.

Our philosophy embraces discipline, respect, communication, and problem-solving skills, woven into everyday learning. We encourage our students to express themselves freely, take initiative, and grow with confidence. Equal importance is given to sports, extracurricular activities, and moral development, ensuring that every child blossoms not just intellectually, but emotionally and socially.
